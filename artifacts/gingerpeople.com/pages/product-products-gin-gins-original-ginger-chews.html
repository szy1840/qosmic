Addictively Delicious

Blakely
04/11/2025

I first encountered these chews back in the day at Walmart. While I don’t remember why I decided to try them, I’m grateful I did, because these are absolutely delectable!Show more

As someone with multiple physical chronic illnesses (including arthritis and IBS) these have to be one of my favorite candies because they DON’T negatively impact my conditions. When I’m craving something sweet (which, as a baker, is often!) these are my go-to, because they satisfy my sweet tooth without causing me any intestinal or joint discomfort. When Walmart stopped carrying this, I was devastated, but thankfully they’re available at my local World Market. I have to be careful though — I can take down a 1lb bag in less than a week if I don’t watch myself!

These chews are sweet without being overly sugary in flavor (and ingredients!). For anyone who’s worried about them being too spicy, I’ve always considered myself a bit of a baby when it comes to spice (you won’t catch me eating buffalo sauce), so while they do give you a mouth tingling sensation, you won’t need milk or starch to put out the fire; even with 10% ginger, they aren’t overwhelmingly spicy. They’re wonderful for nausea, and as someone who loves to sing but has environmental allergies, they help open up my nasal passages as well.

I think my favorite thing about this product is the company behind it; I strive to support smaller businesses when possible, and the fact that these candies are produced using sustainable ingredients grown by farms local to the production factories makes them all the sweeter to me. I place high value in products that give back to the communities where they’re produced, so to know that the company does just that and more means a lot to me. I love sharing these candies with others, and I always keep some on me for anyone dealing with nausea (or just curious to try one!).

I’ll always recommend this brand and their products; even IF you can find cheaper (which, in my opinion, these are very affordable), you won’t find any with a better commitment to making the best responsibly made ginger candies anywhere in store. I absolutely LOVE these!!
