About Us

At Zen Rojas, we believe every sip is a reminder to slow down, breathe deeply, and reconnect with the moment. Our teas are crafted to bring harmony to your day — one mindful cup at a time.

Veteran owned and family operated, Zen Rojas was born from a simple truth: calm is something we create. Through years of service and travel, we discovered how deeply nature can restore balance and peace. That experience shaped our mission — to provide organic, intentional blends that nourish both body and mind.

Every tea we carry is designed to support your wellness — from grounding greens and soothing herbs to uplifting florals that awaken the senses.

At our core, Zen Rojas is more than tea.
It’s a ritual.
A breath.
A return to stillness.

Thank you for sharing a cup with us.
From our family to yours — peace, gratitude, and good tea.
